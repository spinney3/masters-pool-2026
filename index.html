Pool Rules & Instructions
Everything you need to know about the 2026 Masters Pool

Overview

Welcome to the 2026 Masters Pool! Each participant selects 8 golfers split across three tiers (pools) based on their Vegas odds. Your best 5 scores count — the lowest total wins!

Scoring

  • Each golfer's final tournament score (relative to par) counts for your entry.
  • Your best (lowest) 5 scores count — the highest 3 scores are dropped.
  • The entry with the lowest cumulative score from their top 5 golfers wins.
  • Scores update live during tournament rounds.

Cut Rule

  • At least 5 of your 8 golfers must make the cut to remain in the pool.
  • If fewer than 5 make the cut, your entry is eliminated.
  • Golfers who miss the cut receive a score of +10 (penalty) for pool purposes.

Tiebreakers

  • Tiebreaker #1: Closest guess to the actual winning score (to par) wins. Example: if the winner shoots -14, the entry closest to -14 wins the tiebreaker.
  • Tiebreaker #2: In the event of a remaining tie, the average score of your top 5 qualifiers during Sunday's final round determines the winner. (This is automatic — no pick needed.)

Entry & Payment

  • Entry fee: $25 per entry
  • Maximum 2 entries per person
  • Payment via Venmo to @pinneys — include your name in the note
  • Entries must be submitted before the first tee shot on Thursday, April 9

Prize Structure

Prize distribution is determined by the commissioner based on total entries. Typical structure:
1st Place: 60% · 2nd Place: 25% · 3rd Place: 15%
